The MIDI-9: Practical, Reliable, and Built for the Studio

The MIDI-9 was born out of a simple idea: what if there was a MIDI controller that fit perfectly into a modern hybrid studio? No extra clutter, just a solid, well-built tool that worked exactly as you needed. The MIDI-9 is a 19-inch rack-mounted MIDI controller designed for precision and flexibility, offering nine high-quality knobs that adapt to your workflow — whether you need subtle, fine-tuned control or quick, responsive adjustments.

What makes the MIDI-9 stand out isn’t flashy features or complicated specs. It’s the attention to detail. It’s built to integrate smoothly with your existing setup, saving space while providing the control you need. It feels solid, dependable, and perfectly suited for the modern producer or musician.
